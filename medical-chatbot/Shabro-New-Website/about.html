About Shabro Metallics

Shabro Metallics Pvt Ltd is a leading international trading company specializing in steel products, ferrous and non-ferrous scrap, iron ore, pellets and industrial raw materials.

Established in 1994 and headquartered in New Delhi, the company has developed strong global sourcing and distribution networks across Asia, Europe, North America and the Middle East.

As part of the Sharda Group, Shabro Metallics combines decades of industry expertise with a customer-focused approach, delivering reliable solutions to steel manufacturers, traders and industrial customers worldwide.
